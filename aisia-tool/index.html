Section 3 — Impact identification and analysis

For each identified impact, rate Severity (1–4) and Likelihood (1–4). Score = Severity × Likelihood. Consider: Fairness & Non-Discrimination, Privacy, Transparency, Accountability, Safety, Societal impact, Human oversight.

Section 5 — Relationship to GDPR DPIA

Where personal data is processed, DPIA and AISIA may be conducted jointly. DPIA takes precedence for data protection aspects; AISIA extends scope to broader societal and ethical dimensions not covered by DPIA.

ISO/IEC 42001:2023 Annex A.5.2 | GDPR Article 35

Section 6 — Post-deployment monitoring and review plan

Post-deployment monitoring is mandatory for high risk systems. Must detect bias drift, performance degradation, and unintended consequences over time.
